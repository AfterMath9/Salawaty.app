Support & FAQ

How can we help you today?

How are prayer times calculated?

Salawaty uses your device's GPS location to determine astronomical calculations. You can select different calculation methods (e.g., Muslim World League, ISNA) in the app settings to match your region's local masjid.

Is the Qibla compass accurate?

The Qibla compass relies on your device's magnetometer and location services. For best accuracy, ensure you are away from large metal objects or electronic devices that might cause magnetic interference. Calibrate your compass by moving your phone in a figure-eight pattern.

How does Prayer Focus Mode work?

Focus Mode utilizes Apple's Screen Time API to help you minimize distractions. When enabled, it can restrict access to selected apps during prayer windows. No personal app usage data is ever collected or sent to our servers.

How do I set up Home Screen widgets?

Long-press on your Home Screen, tap the '+' button in the top corner, search for 'Salawaty', and select the widget size and type you prefer. Widgets update automatically throughout the day.
