Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Introduction

Salawaty ('we', 'our', or 'us') is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Salawaty mobile application.

Data Collection and Use

Location Data

Salawaty requires access to your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times and determine the Qibla direction. This data is used solely for these purposes and is processed locally on your device.

No User Accounts

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal identification (such as name or email) to use the app.

Data Storage

Any preferences or settings you choose within the app are stored exclusively on your local device. We do not maintain any external servers that store user data.

Third-Party Services

The app fetches content from the following public APIs:

  • hadithapi.com: Used to fetch daily Hadith content.
  • alquran.cloud: Used to fetch Quranic verses and audio.

No user location or personal data is shared with these services. Requests to these APIs are anonymous.

Analytics and Advertising

Salawaty does not include any analytics, tracking software, or third-party advertising.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
